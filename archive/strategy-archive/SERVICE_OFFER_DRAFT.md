# Small Asset License Audit Service Draft

## Offer

Starter Asset Source Audit

## Price

USD $19 launch test

## Buyer Fit

Indie game developers, game jam participants, and prototype builders who already have a small list of free asset/source URLs and want a second pass before publishing.

## Scope

- Up to 10 asset/source URLs.
- Short license-signal review.
- Attribution-required vs CC0/no-attribution flag.
- Redistribution-risk notes.
- Font-specific questions if fonts are included.
- Before-shipping checklist notes.

## Delivery

One short Markdown or PDF-style report.

Suggested delivery time: 48 hours.

## Public Copy

Send me up to 10 asset/source URLs. I will return a short practical audit note with license-signal checks, attribution-risk flags, and before-shipping questions.

This is not legal advice and does not replace a lawyer. It is a practical review pass for indie devs who want fewer surprises before publishing a game jam or prototype build.

## Not Included

- Legal clearance.
- Lawyer-reviewed advice.
- Guaranteed commercial-use approval.
- Downloading or redistributing third-party asset files.
- Reviewing more than 10 URLs in the starter tier.
