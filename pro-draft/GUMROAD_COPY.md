# Game Asset License Tracker Pro Preview

## Short Description

A Google Sheets-ready Pro Preview for indie game developers who use free game assets and need to separate CC0, attribution-required, font-embedding, and redistribution-risk cases quickly.

## Product Promise

Stop treating "free game assets" as one safe bucket.

This tracker helps you sort asset sources by:

- CC0 / public-domain signal
- attribution-required risk
- commercial-use signal
- redistribution risk
- source/provenance notes
- font embedding and modification questions
- Google Sheets-friendly filters

It is built for indie developers, game-jam teams, and solo devs who want to prototype fast without losing track of license obligations.

## Who This Is For

- Indie game developers
- Game jam participants
- Solo devs collecting CC0 / free assets
- Developers who need to track attribution and license notes
- People who have saved too many asset links and do not remember which are actually usable

## Who This Is Not For

- People looking for a pack of redistributed art/audio files
- People who need legal advice
- People who want guaranteed legal clearance
- People who do not want to verify source pages before shipping

## Important Disclaimer

This is not legal advice.

The tracker links to original source pages and does not redistribute third-party asset files.

Before shipping a commercial game, verify each source page and license terms again.
