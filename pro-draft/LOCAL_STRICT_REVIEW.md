# Local Strict Review - Gumroad Pro Draft

Date: 2026-05-27

## Verdict

Status: **needs_revision**

The Gumroad copy and product spec are directionally correct, but the paid product should not be published yet.

Reason:

- The Pro offer promises `150+ reviewed asset/font rows`, but the actual Pro dataset has not been built yet.
- Publishing now would risk selling a promise rather than a finished product.
- The copy correctly avoids selling third-party assets and includes a legal disclaimer.
- The paid value proposition is valid: audit work, filtering, provenance notes, and time saved.

## What Passed

- Does not redistribute third-party asset files.
- Explicitly says this is not legal advice.
- Positions the paid value as audit/structure/filtering, not asset resale.
- Has a clear launch price: USD $9.
- Has a reasonable buyer: indie developers, game-jam teams, solo devs.

## Blocking Issues

1. **Pro dataset missing**
   - Need at least a real Pro preview or Pro v0.1 workbook.
   - Minimum before publishing: 20+ additional rows beyond free version.
   - Better target: 150+ rows as promised.

2. **Paid/free difference not yet real enough**
   - Current paid offer is stronger than the current deliverable.
   - A buyer needs to receive something immediately better than the free GitHub release.

3. **No Gumroad delivery package yet**
   - Need a ZIP or XLSX/CSV bundle that can be uploaded.
   - Need changelog and short buyer README.

## Allowed Next Step

Build **Pro Preview v0.1**:

- 20+ additional reviewed rows beyond the free tracker.
- Dedicated `Pro Preview` workbook.
- Buyer README.
- Changelog.
- Clear label: `Pro Preview`, not final 150-row version.

Then Gumroad can be published as either:

- `Game Asset License Tracker Pro Preview` at USD $5-9, or
- `Game Asset License Tracker Pro - Launch Discount` only if the full Pro dataset is actually included.

## Copy Safety

Safe claims:

- "license tracker"
- "license signals"
- "risk flags"
- "source/provenance notes"
- "not legal advice"
- "verify before shipping"

Unsafe claims:

- "legally safe"
- "guaranteed cleared"
- "safe for commercial use without checking"
- "copyright-free guarantee"

## Final Recommendation

Do not publish Gumroad yet.

Build the Pro deliverable first, then rerun strict review.
