# Audit Notes - Game Jam Asset License Workflow Kit v0.1

## Verdict

Status: `conditional_pass`

This is stronger than a plain resource list because it packages a workflow: source selection, attribution tracking, provenance logging, font checks, and pre-shipping review.

It is still not legal advice and must not be described as legal clearance.

## What Passed

- Built from existing v0.2 tracker data.
- Includes 42 CC0/no-attribution/no-signup starter rows.
- Includes attribution and provenance templates instead of pretending to have broad CC BY coverage.
- Includes 6 font checklist rows.
- Includes before-shipping checks.
- Redistributes 0 third-party asset files.

## Product Risk

- `creator_policy_signal` rows are weaker than page-level signals.
- The attribution tracker is a template, not a verified database of attribution-required assets.
- The product value is workflow/time saved, not ownership of assets.
- Current paid positioning should be a small validation product, not a large Pro product.
